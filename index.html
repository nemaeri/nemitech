Our Story
From the Ministry to the Market

Nemitech Solutions was founded with a simple conviction: Kenya's institutions deserve world-class digital systems built by people who truly understand them — their workflows, their languages, and the Kenyan digital environment they operate in.

The founding team brings deep experience in digital systems delivery across government, health, agriculture, and private sector institutions. We understand M-Pesa, county devolution, Kenyan procurement processes, and what it takes to build digital tools that staff actually use.

Nemitech Solutions has delivered platforms for professional associations, veterinary institutions, agribusiness enterprises, real estate firms, and health bodies — all built with the same rigour: responsive design, M-Pesa integration, cloud infrastructure, and genuine post-launch support.
